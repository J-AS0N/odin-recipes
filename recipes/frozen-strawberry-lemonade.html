Frozen Strawberry Lemonade

The frozen strawberry lemonade is made with fresh ripe strawberries, lemonade, and ice blended into refreshing slushie.

This non-alcoholic, fruity drink is perfect for summer!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 5 large fresh ripe strawberries, hulled

Steps

  1. Place ice cubes, water, and lemonade concentrate into the container of a blender. Process until ice is finely blended.
  2. Add strawberries; puree until well blended
  3. Serve.
